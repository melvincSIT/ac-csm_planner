How to plan

Click a coloured tile to assign a module to that trimester. Click again to remove it.

  • Leave — bottom row; mark trimesters when you are not studying.
  • Career Catalyst — available in any trimester, but not in the same trimester as a micro-credential.
  • Industry Work Experience — programme Year 2 only; available after 3 micro-credentials, or 2 micro-credentials plus Career Catalyst. Once eligible, it stays available in each subsequent Year 2 trimester until selected.
  • Capstone — programme Year 3 only; available after 6 micro-credentials, or 5 micro-credentials plus Career Catalyst.

Landscape orientation

Your plan is formatted for landscape printing. In the print dialog that opens next, set Orientation to Landscape so the timeline fits on one page.

To save as PDF, choose Save as PDF (or similar) as the destination.

Browsers cannot switch orientation automatically — you may need to change it once in the print dialog.
